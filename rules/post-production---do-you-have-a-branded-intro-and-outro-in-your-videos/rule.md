A branded intro and outro will associate the video with your company. Intros are the visuals at the very beginning of your video, while outros appear in the end.

 ![](Screen Shot 2019-05-30 at 8.42.12 AM.png)Good example: Intros can have the video title; event logo; or speaker details (depending on the type of video you're making)![Screen Shot 2019-05-30 at 8.25.51 AM.png](Screen Shot 2019-05-30 at 8.25.51 AM.png)Good example: Outros should have your logo and tagline
