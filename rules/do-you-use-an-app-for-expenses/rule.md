<p>Expense apps are&#160;a timesaver, both for the day to day user, as well as the accountant receiving the expenses.</p><p>​​As a user, all you have to do is take a photo of the receipt from your mobile app and add a comment (all other data is pulled in via OCR “Smart-scan”), then all of these expenses get batched together and sent on a weekly basis to the accountant, who can then manage them in bulk.&#160;<br>Best of all… for light users… it’s free!​&#160;​<img src="/SiteAssets/use-expensify-for-expenses/2020-06-12_12-10-17.png" alt="2020-06-12_12-10-17.png" style="font-weight&#58;bold;background-color&#58;transparent;color&#58;#555555;margin&#58;5px;width&#58;798px;" /><span style="font-weight&#58;bold;">​​</span></p><dd class="ssw15-rteElement-FigureGood">Figure&#58; Good Example – Hubdoc is free with Xero<br>​<br></dd><dl class="goodImage"><p class="ssw15-rteElement-P">For better results claiming your receipts, please read&#160;<a href="/_layouts/15/FIXUPREDIRECT.ASPX?WebId=3dfc0e07-e23a-4cbb-aac2-e778b71166a2&amp;TermSetId=07da3ddf-0924-4cd2-a6d4-a4809ae20160&amp;TermId=48a3a405-7156-4a01-8a4f-a4cd6c82717c">Do you know how to enter a Hubdoc receipt?​</a><br></p><p class="ssw15-rteElement-P">​<br><br></p></dl>
