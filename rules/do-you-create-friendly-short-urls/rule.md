  <p>When you create short URLs, it is worth considering exactly why you are shortening it.</p>
<p> Is it for space or readability? If it is for readability or you expect anyone to ever type it in, then consider making a friendly short URL.<span style="line-height&#58;20px;">​​</span></p>
