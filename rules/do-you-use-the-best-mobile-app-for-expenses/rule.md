<div>
   <br>

      <b>#2 General expenses </b>– when you are at the shop and using the company credit card, you don’t need reimbursement, but you do need to give accounts a photo of the receipt and the purchased item.<br><br></div><div>

<p>Expense apps are&#160;a timesaver, both for the day to day user, as well as the accountant receiving the expenses.</p><p>​​As a user, all you have to do is take a photo of the receipt from your mobile app and add a comment (all other data is pulled in via OCR “Smart-scan”), then all of these expenses get batched together and sent on a weekly basis to the accountant, who can then manage them in bulk.&#160;<br>Best of all… for light users… it’s free!
