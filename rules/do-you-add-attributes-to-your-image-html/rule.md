<p>​What do you do when you want images to link to your site? In the case of images, Google will look for the &quot;alt&quot; text and factor this into the search results. So when you ask someone else to link to your site with an image you should always add attributes like &quot;alt&quot; and &quot;title&quot; into the code. Here's an example.&#160;<br></p>
