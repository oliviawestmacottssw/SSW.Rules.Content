<p>​You should be reimbursed for all reasonable expenses incurred in carrying out your duties, which are above your normal costs, to and from the office.</p><p>A common example is bus and train fares to client sites. Amounts under $40 should be reimbursed through Petty Cash.&#160;In SSW China office, amounts under ￥200 should be reimbursed through Petty Cash.</p><p><br></p>
<br><excerpt class='endintro'></excerpt><br>
