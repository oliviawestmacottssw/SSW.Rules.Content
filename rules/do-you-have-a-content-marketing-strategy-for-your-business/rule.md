Try googling content marketing. You’ll find lots of articles about it, and maybe some reports saying how this has been one of the main marketing strategies in the last 3 years. Content marketing is a strategic marketing approach focused on creating and distributing valuable, relevant, and consistent content to attract and retain a clearly defined audience — and, of course, to drive profitable customer action. That’s why this is so important to be a part of your strategy. In other words&#58; in order to sell the big piece of the pie, you have to show your audience small samples first.
<br><excerpt class='endintro'></excerpt><br>
<div>We are in a new era of content. Not just any content, but a well-crafted and relevant content for your target audience.
