<p>​​​​​​​​​​​​​​In order to get better clarity on what work is done, it's a good idea to connect your PBI's and tasks to the commits, branches and pull requests&#160;that relate to the item.&#160;All commits, branches and pull requests should be linked to a&#160;PBI.​​<br></p><dl class="badImage"><dt><img src="/SiteAssets/do-you-link-your-commits-to-a-pbi/no-linked-commit.png" alt="no-linked-commit.png" /></dt><dd>​Bad Example&#58; No linked commit's, branches or pull requests<br></dd>​​</dl><dl class="goodImage"><dt><img src="/SiteAssets/do-you-link-your-commits-to-a-pbi/link-branch-to-pbi.png" alt="linked-commit" /></dt><dd>Good Example&#58; Git branch linked to PBI in&#160;TFS<br></dd>
</dl>
<br><excerpt class='endintro'></excerpt><br>
