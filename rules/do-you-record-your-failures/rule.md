<div>While developing a feature or fix, it is important to show the attempts you make. Experimentation history is not recorded​<br></dd></div><div><br><div><img src="/SiteAssets/do-you-record-you-failures/commit-failed-experiments.png" alt="commit-failed-experiments.png" style="margin&#58;5px;width&#58;508px;" />​<br></div><div><dd class="ssw15-rteElement-FigureGood">Good Example&#58; Each attempt has a new commit and is not lost when retrieving history<br></dd></div></div>
